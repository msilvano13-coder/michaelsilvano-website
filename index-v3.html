Hey, I’m Michael

I’m a Boston-based wedding photographer who blends film and digital to create images that feel timeless, warm, and genuinely you. My approach is calm, detail-oriented, and entirely focused on preserving the moments that matter most.

I believe the best wedding photos come from real connection — not stiff posing. When you feel at ease, everything falls into place naturally, and that’s exactly the kind of energy I bring to every wedding day.

When I’m not behind the camera, you’ll find me exploring New England, trying a new coffee spot, or hanging with my dog. I’d love to hear your story.
