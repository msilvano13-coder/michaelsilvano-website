The Photographer

Hello, I'm Michael

I believe the best photographs happen when people forget there's a camera in the room. My work lives in the space between posed and candid — observing carefully, anticipating meaningful moments, and honoring the energy of your day as it naturally unfolds.

Rooted in New England and drawn to honest storytelling, I blend the warmth of film with the clarity of digital to create images that feel both immediate and timeless. Whether it's the quiet of a first look or the chaos of a packed dance floor, I'm there for all of it.
